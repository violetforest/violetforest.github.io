~* Violet Forest *~

I'm an artist working solo & collaboratively. I co-founded Cybertwee with Gabriella Hileman & May Waver, a collective that explores the intersections of technology & cuteness. You might remember my solo project, Vickie, an ongoing photo-essay of my sister that I started in 2011. I used to be a freelance front-end developer in Chicago, then I lived in Berlin and worked at Volkswagen Future Design Center Europe doing UX Design and prototyping for self-driving vehicles. Now I'm back in Miami working on art full-time making NFTs, and sometimes take freelance projects :)
